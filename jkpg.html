Jönköping is a beautiful place to live. From the first day of my move, I knew that I would enjoy it here. Not only is the scenery jaw dropping, but there's so much to do, especially as a student.

Things to do here

  • go shopping along Östra Storgatan or the large mall, Asecs, that's right outside of centrum
  • attend Akademien, a student nightclub that hosts on Wednesdays
  • take a stroll or bike alongside the southern part of Vättern, Sweden's second largest inland body of water
  • make your way out to Elmia, a convention center, for different showcases, hockey games, and other sports
  • Shooters billiards is a fun place to spend an evening with food, drinks, billiards, darts, and shuffleboard to accompany you
  • next door to Shooters is a karaoke bar where you can sing your heart out to your favourite tunes
  • if you fancy a trip a bit more northern, Gränna has all of the candy shops you could ever ask for
  • a ferry from Gränna takes you to an island called Visingsö, which is full of history, ice cream, mini golf, and horse wagon tours

Yummy bites in town

  • centrum has an abundance of burger joints, so whether you choose Burger King, Bröderna, Bada Bing Burger, or Bastard Burgers, you're getting a satisfying meal!
  • if all you can eat is more your style, Basilika is an Asian-inspired buffet that offers student discounts on the entire display
  • Mr. Rice also offers a student discount and an array of delicious Asian cuisine
  • Lebanese cuisine is always delicious and Samboosak never fails to deliver. Not only are the portion sizes enough to last you two more meals, but the flavour within every side dish is to die for!
  • Pizzeria Alcamo has pizzas with as many toppings as you want and a salad bar where you can make your own
